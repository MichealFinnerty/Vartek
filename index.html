Questions

Frequently asked questions

Are your alarm systems monitored 24 hours a day?

Yes. Our intruder alarm systems offer 24 hour IP alarm monitoring over GSM, with smartphone notifications and control so you know what's happening at your property at any time.

Do you offer maintenance after installation?

Yes. We provide follow-up and after-sales service, including telephone support, and maintenance contracts are available for all the systems we install.

Can I view my CCTV footage remotely?

Yes. Our Hikvision CCTV systems work with the Hik-Connect smartphone app, giving you remote viewing of your cameras while you're away from your premises.

Do you install for residential and commercial properties?

Yes. All three of our systems, CCTV, intruder alarms and access control, are supplied and installed for residential, commercial and industrial properties.

What areas do you cover?

We're based in Askeaton, Co. Limerick, and cover Limerick city and the wider Munster area. Get in touch to check we cover your location.
